Playing and audio sources

Audio clips are easily playable, even without using the Type method, as long as you have an audio clip.

You can call the Play method of an DialogueAudioSource object and pass in the clip as a parameter. And to change the volume of the audio, just change the volume property of the audio source. 

JavaScript

Audios played using the system are stackable and will not stop each other. Also take note that once audio is played, it cannot be paused or stopped.
