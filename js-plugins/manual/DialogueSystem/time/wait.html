Waiting for time

With events and stories or anything with dialogues and text, there's always a pause. It may be short, it may be long, but there's still a pause.

To add pauses between tasks and executions, you can use the DialogueLoop.Delay method. The Delay method is asynchronous, so you have to call it within an asynchronous environment. Await a Delay method call on the part you want to get a pause and pass in the amount of time in seconds you want to wait for.
